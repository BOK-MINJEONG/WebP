What is Artificial Intelligence?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is a field of computer science dedicated to solving cognitive problems commonly associated with human intelligence, such as learning, creating, and recognizing images. AI aims to develop self-learning systems that derive meaning from massive amounts of data collected from various sources. For instance, AI technologies can meaningfully respond to human conversation, create original images and texts, and make decisions based on real-time data input.

Artificial intelligence can offer a wide range of benefits across various industries. Key benefits include:

  • Solving complex problems
  • Increasing business efficiency
  • Smart decision-making
  • Automating business processes
